Domains
Your own properties, target domains, and domains to ignore.
💡
Queries moved to Scripts. Search terms are now configured directly inside a loop step on the Scripts page — one query list per loop, fully customizable. That lets you run different queries in different rotations, with different per-ad pipelines, in one script.
🏠 My domains
Domains you own and don't want to count as competitors. Ads matching any of these entries are excluded from the Competitors table. Step-level skip_on_my_domain checks use this list too — handy for not clicking your own ads and burning your ad budget.
🎯 Target domains
Ads that match any domain here trigger the on-target script instead of the default post-click pipeline. Use for competitors you want to treat specially — e.g. longer dwell time or different interaction patterns.
🚫 Block list
optional
Domains to skip entirely — the monitor sees them in the SERP but doesn't record, click, or otherwise interact. Useful for massive aggregators (marketplaces, news sites) that dilute your stats.
